About US

Our mission




As we live through unprecedented times, we believe that it is our duty to support the ones who have lost the most. As small businesses have lost revenue because of insufficient funding, we hope to provide a platform that biusinesses can promote their products and consumers can interact with them.



We hope to provide a platform that not only helps build up small businesses and help them during these hard times, but also a place where the public can easily support these businesses.
